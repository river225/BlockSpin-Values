Privacy Policy

BlockSpin Values ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at https://blockspinvalues.com/ and our Discord bot / community tools connected to that service.

1. Information We Collect

You can browse most of the website without creating an account. Some optional features require Discord login.

Depending on how you use our services, we may process:

  • Discord account data (when you choose to log in): Discord user ID, username, display name / global name, and avatar URL, obtained through Discord OAuth
  • Saved cards: item identifiers and display metadata you choose to bookmark (for example item name, image URL, section)
  • Contact information you voluntarily send us (email or Discord username) if you message us
  • Technical / hosting data such as IP address, browser type, and pages requested, which may appear in hosting or server logs
  • Discord bot operational data described in Section 6

We do not collect payment card information. The website is free to use.

2. Discord Login (Optional)

Optional Discord login lets you use features such as Saved Cards across devices.

  • Login is handled through Discord OAuth. We do not receive or store your Discord password.
  • After you authorize the app, our backend issues a signed session token and returns it to the website.
  • Your browser stores that session token in localStorage under a key such as bsv-discord-auth so you stay signed in.
  • We may also temporarily store a return URL (where to send you after login) in sessionStorage / localStorage or a short-lived cookie during the OAuth flow.
  • You can log out from the website. Clearing site data in your browser also removes the local session token.

Discord’s own processing is governed by Discord’s Privacy Policy.

3. Saved Cards

Saved Cards lets logged-in users bookmark items from the value list (subject to a maximum, currently 60 cards).

  • Server storage: When you are logged in and our API is available, saved cards are stored on our bot backend (hosted with providers such as Railway) in per-user files keyed by Discord user ID.
  • Local fallback: If the server is unavailable, cards may also be kept temporarily in your browser under keys like bsv-saved-cards-local-{userId} and synced when possible.
  • What is stored: Discord user ID plus card metadata needed to restore the bookmark (not your Discord password or message history).
  • Use: Only to show and manage your saved items on the website.
  • Deletion: Remove cards in the Saved Cards UI, or contact us to request deletion of your saved-card file. Logging out does not automatically wipe server-side saved cards unless you delete them or request account/data deletion.

4. Browser Storage (localStorage / sessionStorage)

We store a small amount of data on your device (not always on our servers). Examples include:

  • bsv-discord-auth — Discord session token (if logged in)
  • bsv-oauth-return-to — temporary return URL during Discord login
  • bsv-saved-cards-local-* — local saved-card fallback
  • bsv-lang — language preference
  • bsv-font — font preference
  • bsv-theme — theme preference (if enabled)
  • bsv-discord-nudge-dismissed — whether you dismissed a Discord join reminder (session)

Third-party ad scripts (such as Google AdSense) may set additional storage under their own policies. You can clear site data anytime in your browser settings.

5. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, local storage, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to show and measure ads and, where allowed, to personalize ads.

See How Google uses cookies in advertising, Google’s Privacy Policy, and Google Ads Settings.

6. Discord Bot and Community Tools

Our Discord application (“BSV Bot”) operates in the BlockSpin Values Discord server and related systems. Depending on the feature, it may process:

  • Server member / role data: to assign or remove roles (for example server-tag roles and Middleman Client status roles), check staff permissions, announce boosts, and support website middleman listings
  • Message activity in specific channels: for example, counting messages in our vouch channel to update client-status roles. For that feature we store Discord user IDs and numeric message counts, not a full archive of message text
  • Message content in limited features: such as reacting to W/F/L keywords in a designated channel, or operating middleman/support ticket flows. Content is read to provide the feature; we do not use message content to train AI/ML models
  • Ticket / support operational records: as needed to run middleman and support workflows

Bot data is stored on our hosting provider for those features to keep working after restarts. We do not sell this data. Users can limit participation by not using those channels/features, leaving the server, or contacting us / server staff about role or data concerns.

7. Hosting and Technical Information

The website may be hosted on services such as GitHub Pages. The Discord bot / API may be hosted on services such as Railway. Providers may log technical information for security and operations.

Your browser may also contact third parties we rely on (Google Sheets, Discord, image hosts, Roblox avatar helpers). Those services receive standard request data under their own policies.

8. How Website Features Use Your Device

Tax calculator and item search: Run in your browser. We do not store calculator inputs or searches on our servers as a personal profile.

Spreadsheet content: Values are fetched in your browser from public Google Sheets (read-only).

Discord invite counts / boosters: Public Discord or bot API requests may be made to display member counts or booster lists.

9. Richest Players Leaderboard

Our "Richest Players" feature displays in-game wealth rankings:

  • Official Leaderboard: BlockSpin's official richest players leaderboard maintained by the game's community management
  • Data Source: Player usernames, in-game levels and cash amounts from an official Google Sheet regulated by BlockSpin's Community Lead, Csnsor
  • Roblox Profiles: Clicking a player name links to their public Roblox profile
  • Public Information: Displayed usernames and cash amounts are already publicly visible in-game
  • Removal Requests: Contact BlockSpin's Community Lead Csnsor, or contact us for guidance

10. Third-Party Services and Links

We load data from and link to external services, including Google Sheets, Google AdSense, Discord, TikTok, Roblox, and image hosts (for example ImgBB). Their privacy policies apply when you use those services.

11. How We Use Data

We use the information above to:

  • Operate the website, Discord bot, tickets, roles, and giveaways
  • Provide Discord login and Saved Cards
  • Remember preferences on your device
  • Show ads (via Google) and measure basic site operations
  • Respond to support requests and protect against abuse

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use Discord message content to train machine learning or AI models.

12. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal data. You can:

  • Log out and clear site data in your browser
  • Delete Saved Cards in the website UI
  • Manage ads via Google Ads Settings
  • Contact us (Section 14) to request deletion of Discord-login session records or saved-card data we control
  • Leave or adjust participation in Discord features / ask server staff about roles
