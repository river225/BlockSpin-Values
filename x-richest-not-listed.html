Not on the Richest Players Leaderboard?

If you do not appear on the Richest Players leaderboard, it is usually due to one of the reasons below.

  1. 1

    Account not verified

    To appear on the leaderboard, your Roblox account must be verified in the official BlockSpin Discord server. Join the server and complete verification to become eligible.

  2. 2

    Inventory value below the cutoff

    The leaderboard ranks the top 1,000 players by total in-game asset value. If your combined inventory value is below the threshold for the 1,000th-ranked player, you will not appear on the list.
